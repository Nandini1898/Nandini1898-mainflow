Organic Farming

Understanding Organic Agriculture

Organic farming is a method of agriculture that emphasizes the use of natural processes and materials. It avoids synthetic fertilizers and pesticides, focusing instead on sustainability and environmental health.

Key principles of organic farming include:

  • Enhancing biodiversity
  • Maintaining soil health
  • Using crop rotation and cover crops
